"Queen of Hills"



SAJEK VALLEY




Sajek Valley is located in the Rangamati District, in the northern Chittagong Hill Tracts, among the hills of the Kasalong range of mountains. The valley offers a rare blend of lush green hills, dense forests, and panoramic views of cloud-laden peaks that seem to float within reach. As the "Queen of Hills," Sajek is perfect for nature lovers, photographers, and adventurers seeking tranquility and scenic beauty.
